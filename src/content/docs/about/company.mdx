EndoTech Singapore brings advanced endodontic instrumentation to clinicians across the Asia-Pacific region.

## Our Focus

We specialise in rotary file systems that integrate seamlessly with established clinical workflows. Our products are designed for dentists who:

- Value predictable clinical outcomes
- Want to maintain their existing technique
- Demand quality instrumentation

## TransformX Technology

Our TransformX files feature two key innovations:

### Transform Technology
Optimised nickel-titanium phase transition along the file length provides:
- Enhanced flexibility in curved canals
- Improved torsional stability
- Superior fatigue resistance

### Avatar™ Tip
A proprietary tip design that:
- Promotes centred canal preparation
- Reduces transportation risk
- Minimises procedural errors
