The TransformX system follows a straightforward 3-step workflow designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing technique.

## The 3-Step Workflow

### Step 1: Glide Path
Establish a reproducible glide path to working length.

### Step 2: Shaping
Use the TransformX sequence appropriate for your system:
- **ET system**: For .04/.06 taper workflows
- **PT system**: For progressive taper workflows

### Step 3: Finishing
Complete the preparation with final apical sizing as needed.

## Key Principles

**Irrigate → Recapitulate → Re-irrigate**

Between each file:
1. Irrigate thoroughly
2. Recapitulate with a small hand file
3. Re-irrigate before the next rotary file

This sequence ensures debris removal and reduces the risk of blockage or ledging.
