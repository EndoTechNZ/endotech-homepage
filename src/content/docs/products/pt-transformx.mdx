TransformX™ PT files are engineered for clinicians experienced with ProTaper Gold or ProTaper Next systems, preserving a familiar progressive shaping philosophy while elevating clinical precision.

## Key Features

- **Transform Technology** optimises phase transition along the file, enhancing flexibility, torsional control, and fatigue resistance
- **Avatar™ Tip** promotes centred apical preparation, minimising transportation and iatrogenic risk
- Refined canal centring and controlled shaping dynamics
- Reproducible obturation conditions
- Seamlessly integrates into established PT workflows without altering technique or motor parameters

## Clinical Benefits

- Familiar progressive shaping philosophy
- Enhanced clinical precision over standard PT systems
- Compatible with your existing motor settings
