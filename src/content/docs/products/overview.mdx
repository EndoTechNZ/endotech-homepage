EndoTech Singapore offers a focused range of advanced endodontic instruments designed for predictable clinical outcomes.

## TransformX™ Rotary Files

Our TransformX files feature **Transform Technology** — optimised phase transition along the file that enhances flexibility, torsional stability, and fatigue resistance.

The **Avatar™ Tip** promotes centred apical progression, reducing transportation and procedural error.
