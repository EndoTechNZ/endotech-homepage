**GOLD STANDARD** — iRoot® SP is a premixed, injectable bioceramic root canal sealer that uses the natural moisture within dentinal tubules to initiate setting.

<Image src={irootProduct} alt="iRoot Sealer" width={600} />

## Key Features

- **Premixed and injectable** — ready to use, no mixing required
- **Highly radiopaque** — excellent visibility on radiographs
- **Hydrophilic** — uses natural moisture to set
- **Forms hydroxyapatite** upon setting
- **Chemical bonding** to dentin and bioceramic points
- **Antibacterial properties** during curing
- **Zero shrinkage** for reliable 3-D bonded obturation

## Clinical Benefits

- Simplified obturation workflow
- Reliable seal with chemical bonding
- Excellent biocompatibility
- Long-term stability
