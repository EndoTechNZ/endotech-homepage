TransformX™ ET files are engineered for clinicians familiar with EdgeTaper, EdgeX7, Race Evo, and other .04/.06 taper systems, preserving established shaping protocols while advancing clinical control.

<Image src={etProduct} alt="ET TransformX Files" width={600} />

## Key Features

- **Transform Technology** enhances phase transition along the file, optimising flexibility, torsional stability, and fatigue resistance
- **Avatar™ Tip** promotes centred apical progression, reducing transportation and procedural error
- Controlled taper development and predictable canal centring
- Reproducible obturation conditions
- Seamlessly integrates into existing ET-style workflows without changing technique or motor settings

## Clinical Benefits

- Get to working length faster with less separation risk
- Maintains canal centring throughout the shaping sequence
- Compatible with your existing motor settings
