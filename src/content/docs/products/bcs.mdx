The BCS BioCeramics family groups EndoTech's premixed bioceramic materials into one platform for sealing and repair workflows.

The family is presented around two main roles:

- canal sealing with a premixed bioceramic sealer
- repair-oriented procedures with a premixed bioceramic putty

## Product Roles

### BCS BioCeramic Sealer

BCS BioCeramic Sealer is positioned for obturation workflows where clinicians want a premixed calcium silicate-based sealer with moisture-assisted setting.

### BCS BioCeramic Putty

BCS BioCeramic Putty is positioned for repair procedures such as perforation repair, apexification, root-end filling, and related clinical situations where handling and placement control matter.

## Workflow Context

Within the wider EndoTech system story, BCS materials sit after shaping and cleaning:

- Acrobat
- TransformX
- VortiFlow
- BCS

That framing keeps the family connected to the sequence of preparation, irrigation, and final sealing or repair.
